Rugby
  • Rugby is a sport which consists of 2 teams of 15 players, trying to score the most points within 80 minutes. The methods of scoring include: a try (5 points), a conversion (2 points), a penalty (3 points) or a drop goal (3 points).
  • It is a very physical sport as the team in possession of the ball run directly into the defending team, attempting to touch the ball over the try line.
  • The defending team must tackle the opposing player to the ground and attempt to steal the ball in the ruck that is formed.
  • I play for my local club, Bandon RFC at mens level, and a team based in Cork City, Highfield RFC at U20's.

GAA
  • GAA is composed of 2 sports, hurling & football.
  • Both sports are very similar in nature. 2 team of 15 players aim to score the most points in 70 minutes. The methods of scoring include : a goal (3 points) or a point (1 point).
  • The differences between the two sports are : Hurling is played with a sliotar, a small hard ball, football is played with a large leather ball; hurlers must use a hurley, a long stick to strike the ball, and a helmet, footballers are only required to wear gum shields with the option of wearing gloves for additional grip.
  • I play for my local club, Bandon GAA at Junior A level and U20's.
